## Hypertension Protocol
**Diet**: DASH Diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension).
- Low sodium intake (< 2300mg/day).
- High potassium foods (bananas, spinach).
- Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.
**Exercise**: Moderate aerobic activity.
- 30 minutes of brisk walking, 5 days a week.
- Light resistance training (avoid heavy lifting that spikes BP).
**Lifestyle**:
- Limit alcohol consumption.
- Quit smoking.
- Manage stress via meditation or deep breathing (10 mins/day).
**Sleep**: Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep.

## Diabetes Type 2 Daily Routine
**Diet**: Low Glycemic Index (GI) focus.
- Consistent meal timing to regulate blood sugar.
- High fiber intake (vegetables, whole grains).
- Limit simple carbohydrates and sweets.
**Exercise**: Physical activity helps insulin sensitivity.
- Post-meal walks (15-20 mins) are highly effective.
- Strength training 2-3 times a week.
**Lifestyle**:
- Foot care: Inspect feet daily for cuts or sores.
- Hydration: Drink plenty of water.
**Sleep**: 7-8 hours. Lack of sleep can increase insulin resistance.

## Post-Surgery Recovery (General)
**Diet**: High protein for tissue repair.
- Lean meats, eggs, dairy, or plant-based proteins.
- Vitamin C and Zinc rich foods for wound healing.
**Exercise**: Mobility focused.
- Gentle stretching as prescribed by PT.
- Short, frequent walks to prevent blood clots.
- Avoid strenuous activity until cleared.
**Lifestyle**:
- Wound care: Keep incision dry and clean.
- Pain management: Take meds as prescribed, do not "push through" pain.
**Sleep**: Rest is critical. Allow for naps during the day.

## Heart Failure Protocol
**Diet**: Fluid and Salt Restriction.
- Limit fluids to 1.5 - 2 Liters/day (as per doctor).
- Strictly low salt (< 2000mg/day).
**Exercise**: Symptom-limited activity.
- Walk at a comfortable pace. Stop if short of breath.
- Daily weight monitoring: Weigh yourself every morning after voiding.
**Lifestyle**:
- Elevate legs if swollen.
- Avoid temperature extremes.
**Sleep**: Use extra pillows if short of breath while lying flat.
